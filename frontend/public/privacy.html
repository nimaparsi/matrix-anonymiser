Privacy policy

Protect privacy,
everywhere you work.

SanitiseAI is designed as a privacy layer before text is shared with AI tools, docs, or internal workflows. This policy explains how processing works and what data is never stored.

Effective date: March 21, 2026

Privacy principles

  • No raw text storageSubmitted source text and sanitised output are not retained as user history.
  • Transient processingRequests are processed in-memory for sanitisation and then discarded.
  • User controlYou decide what to submit and what to share externally.

How text is processed

Submitted text is analysed to detect and replace sensitive entities. Processing is scoped to the active sanitisation request.

What we do not store

  • Original text content.
  • Sanitised output content.
  • Detected entity values.

Operational telemetry

We retain aggregate service metrics such as request counts, latency, and error rates. Raw request bodies are not intentionally logged.

Rate limiting and abuse prevention

Free-tier limits may use a salted hashed key derived from request metadata. Raw IP addresses are not stored as user identity records.

Payments and third-party processors

Payment checkout is handled by Stripe under Stripe's policies. SanitiseAI does not store full payment card details.

Your controls

Review sanitised output before sharing with third-party systems. You are responsible for final content decisions after anonymisation.

Privacy questions: contact via github.com/nimaparsi/matrix-anonymiser or nimaparsi@icloud.com.
