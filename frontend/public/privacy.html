Privacy policy

How SanitiseAI handles your text

SanitiseAI is built as a privacy-first text sanitisation tool. This policy explains what is processed for each request, what is retained for operational reliability, and what is not stored.

Effective date: March 21, 2026

How text is processed

Submitted text is processed to detect and replace sensitive entities. Processing is transient and scoped to the anonymisation request.

What we do not store

  • Original text content.
  • Sanitised output content.
  • Detected entity values.

Operational telemetry

We retain aggregate metrics for service health, such as request counts, latency, and error codes. Raw request bodies are not intentionally logged.

Rate limiting and abuse prevention

Free-tier limits use a salted hashed key derived from request metadata. Raw IP addresses are not stored as user identity records.

Payments and third-party processors

Payment checkout is handled by Stripe under Stripe's policies. SanitiseAI does not store full payment card details.

Your controls

You choose when text is submitted and what is shared externally. Always review sanitised output before sharing with third-party tools.

For privacy questions, contact the project owner via github.com/nimaparsi/matrix-anonymiser.
