Privacy Policy

Sanitise AI anonymises sensitive text before sharing. This page explains what is processed, what is retained, and what is never stored.

Effective date: March 11, 2026

How text is processed

Submitted text is processed to detect and replace sensitive entities. Processing is transient and scoped to the anonymisation request.

What we do not store

  • Original text content.
  • Anonymised output content.
  • Detected entity values.

Operational telemetry

We retain aggregate metrics for service health, such as request counts, latency, and error codes. Raw request bodies are not intentionally logged.

Rate limiting and abuse prevention

Free-tier limits use a salted hashed key derived from request metadata. Raw IP addresses are not stored as user identity records.

Payments and third-party processors

Payment checkout is handled by Stripe under Stripe's policies. Sanitise AI does not store full payment card details.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the project owner via the open-source repository: github.com/nimaparsi/matrix-anonymiser.
