Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 7, 2026

What we process

Submitted text is processed transiently for anonymisation. We do not store original or anonymised text in our application database.

Usage and billing

Free-tier limits are enforced using a salted hash key derived from request metadata. We do not store raw IP addresses as identity records.

Billing is handled by Stripe. Stripe may process billing email and payment details under its own terms.

Logging and telemetry

We store aggregate operational telemetry only (request count, latency, error code). We do not intentionally log request bodies.
